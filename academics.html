Little Champs an exclusive junior wing of Bhashyam offers a unique Pre-Primary and Primary Years Programme that provides the right foundation for young students right from their impressionable age.

FACULTY

A Smart Way to Learn.

Bhashyam is blessed with one of the best talented pool of teaching fraternity. Their focus is always on developing the individual potential of students in a supportive and academically challenging environment.

The Bhashyam teachers follow a unique high quality academic programs that are aimed at constructing knowledge, skills and understanding of the students through integrating new experiences with their prior learning phase. The faculty with extensive teaching experience use the latest gizmos provided in the campus to make learning easy, interesting and effective. Students at each level are immersed in a range of challenging outdoor experiences aimed at developing character, resilience and personal integrity. At every level, teachers address the anxiety of students and motivate them to perform well both in scholastic and co-scholastic activities.
