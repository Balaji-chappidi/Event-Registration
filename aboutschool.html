Bhashyam EDU Initiatives

Bhashyam started its journey in 1993 with 186 students, with pioneering educational concepts to students from Grade VI to X. As it grew in name and stature, it established exclusive Primary wing schools. It later started innovative foundation courses for aspiring students in Engineering and Medical streams. Today, the group has emerged as the largest educational group with over 1,41,000 students across the various portals of Bhashyam.

Bhashyam

Bhashyam Public School, since its inception is known for its success-oriented programme for Grade VI to X. The curriculum imparted in these schools has been upgraded to suit the new competitive education pattern prevailing in the country. The focused teaching-learning approach followed here makes it a numero uno institution in South India.

Bhashyam Little Champs Winner all the way

To set a strong learning foundation from the formative years itself, the group has set up Bhashyam Little Champs with classes from KG to Grade V. The exclusivelydesigned programs offered here set a perfect launchpad for children to aim for higher levels of knowledge and learning after primary education.

BIIT Bhashyam's IIT Foundation Academy

Bhashyam IIT Foundation Academy delivers a focused and result-oriented 5-year integrated foundation program from Grade VI onwards to nurture students towards success in JEE (Main, Advanced), BITSAT, IIST, KVPY and NEST.

Bhashyam Medi Aims

To suit the new Medical competitive education format in the country, Bhashyam initiated MEDIAIMS, a dedicated Medical Excellence Programme that is aimed to groom students for NEET from their VIII Grade.
